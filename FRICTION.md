# Friction Journal

One bullet per moment of friction encountered while using modeler against the NBA dataset. Every moment you wanted to escape the modeler to build a derived view gets logged here.

Each entry captures:
- **Question:** what were you trying to answer?
- **Friction:** what hurt? (had to leave the app, agent struggled, query was slow, result was wrong, etc.)
- **Wished-for path:** what should have happened within modeler?

The signal this journal produces becomes the de-facto product spec for the writeback-to-Snowflake / derived-concept features.
